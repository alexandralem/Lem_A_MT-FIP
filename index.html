Welcoming Statement

Welcome to Old Spice Brewery!

We have been among the world leaders in beer manufacturing for 25 years. We pay tribute to beer producers who served the Royal Danish Court. And we have maintained our quality ever since. Join our friendly community and choose your own favourite type of beer.

Statistics Data about Product's Popularity

Old Spice has strong market positions globally

We have a well-diversified geographic footprint with strong no. 1 or 2 positions in 25 markets across Western Europe, Asia and Eastern Europe. Around 75% of volumes are sold in these markets and no market accounts for more than 16% of Group volumes.
